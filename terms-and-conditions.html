POPIA Consent & Data Sharing Agreement

Terms and Conditions

Last updated: 27 March 2026

1 Purpose of Collection

By registering a profile or submitting your CV, you ("the Data Subject") acknowledge and agree that JobLynk will collect and process your personal and special personal information (as defined by POPIA). This data is collected specifically for the purpose of:

  • Evaluating your suitability for current and future career opportunities.
  • Facilitating the recruitment process by matching your profile with job specifications.
  • Verifying your credentials (including education, criminal record, and credit history where relevant).

2 Disclosure to Third Parties

You expressly consent to JobLynk sharing your personal information with:

Potential Employers / Clients
We will share your CV, skills profile, and assessment results with companies seeking to fill vacancies.
Training Providers
For the purposes of contacting you with self-development and training opportunities.
Verification Agencies
Third-party service providers used to conduct background checks and qualification verifications.
Technical Partners
Our IT and software providers (e.g., ATS and CRM systems) who host our database securely.

3 Limitation on Further Processing

JobLynk will not use your personal data for any purpose other than recruitment and career-related services without your additional consent.

4 Data Retention & Security

Duration
Your information will be stored on our secure database for a period of 5 years from the date of your last interaction or until you request its removal. This ensures we can contact you for future opportunities.
Security
We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or destruction.

5 Your Rights (The Data Subject)

In accordance with POPIA, you have the right to:

Access
Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
Rectify
Update or correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
Object / Withdraw
Withdraw your consent or object to the processing of your data at any time (subject to legal or contractual obligations).
Delete
Request the permanent deletion of your profile from our systems.
By using JobLynk, you confirm that:
You have read and understood this POPIA Consent & Data Sharing Agreement. You consent to JobLynk processing your personal data and sharing your profile with potential employers for recruitment purposes in line with POPIA.
